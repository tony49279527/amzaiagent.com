Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about accurate competitor analysis

How long does it take to generate a report?

+

Usually, a complete deep analysis report takes only 15-30 minutes to generate, gathering data from thousands of reviews and listing details.

Which Amazon marketplaces are supported?

+

We support 8 Amazon marketplaces: United States, United Kingdom, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Canada, and Japan.

How do you ensure data accuracy?

+

We crawl real-time front-end data ensuring 100% freshness. We then use advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) for multi-dimensional cross-verification, comparing review sentiments with product specifications to ensure analysis accuracy.

Can I export the reports?

+

Yes! You can export the generated reports as PDF format for convenient sharing with your team or archiving for future reference.

Is there a free option available?

+

Yes! Our Free tier lets you generate a market analysis report at no cost. No registration required — just enter your product category and keywords. For deeper analysis with custom prompts, file uploads, and premium AI models, you can upgrade to our Pro tier on a per-report basis.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through Stripe. PayPal support is coming soon. Pro reports are purchased individually — there are no recurring subscriptions.

Can I analyze products outside of Amazon?

+

Currently, our AI models are specialized for Amazon's ecosystem to ensure the highest quality of analysis. We plan to expand to additional e-commerce platforms in future updates.

How does the Pro pricing work?

+

Pro reports are purchased individually at $29.99 per report. You pay a $4.99 deposit to start the analysis, then pay the remaining $25.00 only after reviewing a preview of the report. No subscription or recurring charges.
